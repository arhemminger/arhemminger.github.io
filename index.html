A little bit about me:

I am an aspiring web developer working toward a bachelors in web development at Bellevue University. Experience coding with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PowerShell. Currently working at Nebraska Furniture Mart as a Test Automation Lead in the Software and Applications Development department utilizing the Microsoft stack. Following the Agile methodology with a focus on quality and automation. Transiting into the web development industry 2.5 years ago I really found a new passion for a career field. My background prior was electronics installation which involved some different types of coding to configure whole home automation systems which gave me the experience to put my foot in the door to become a software tester for the Nebraska Furniture Mart website. I work with a great team of people that are always willing to collaborate and share knowledge with one another helping to grow my skill-sets in all areas surrounding web development. I am striving toward completing my degree in web development at Bellevue University to help accelerate my career and become a better developer.
